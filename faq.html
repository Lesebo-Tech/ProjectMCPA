Frequently Asked Questions

What is your ticket refund policy?
Tickets can be refunded up to 48 hours before the event. A small processing fee may apply. For cancellations within 48 hours of the event, we offer credit for future events.
How will I receive my tickets?
All tickets are delivered digitally via email immediately after purchase. You can also access them through your Swift Ticket account or mobile app.
Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?
Yes! You can easily transfer tickets to friends through our app or website. Just go to "My Tickets" and select the "Transfer" option.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cryptocurrency (Bitcoin and Ethereum). Some events may also offer installment payment plans.
What if an event is cancelled?
In case of event cancellation, you'll automatically receive a full refund to your original payment method within 5-7 business days. We'll also notify you via email and SMS.
How do I contact customer support?
Our support team is available 24/7. Call us at (888) 555-7890, email support@swiftticket.com, or use the live chat in our app. Average response time is under 5 minutes!
